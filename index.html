Bio

Hi! I'm a strategist passionate about spreading empathy and joy to as many people as possible.

Currently, I am a Business Associate at Techstars. Prior, I was on the Disney Accelerator team at The Walt Disney Company. Born and raised in the greater Los Angeles area, I have attained multiple corporate strategy experiences in the media and entertainment industry in the private and nonprofit sectors. At USC, I was awarded The Order of the Torch given to 97 out of 5,000 undergraduates of the graduating class for leadership and community service recognition. Previously, I served as an ambassador (selected as 1/7 out of 1,000 women) for the Tournament of Roses.

From a young age, I have cherished globalization and have visited over 25 countries. I consider Indonesia and Singapore my second homes and speak 4 languages. In my free time, I channel my inner Anthony Bourdain and write food reviews of LA restaurants and beyond.
